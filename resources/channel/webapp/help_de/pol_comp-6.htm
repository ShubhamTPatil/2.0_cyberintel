Policy compliance requires the Scanner Service and Policy Service channels on the endpoints (also called managed nodes). If you followed the instructions for creating profiles and installers for the managed nodes, as described in the BMC Configuration Management Installation and Deployment Guide, available on the BMC Customer Support web site, you should have the following channels included in your managed nodes:

    • Infrastructure Service
    • Scanner Service
    • Policy Service
